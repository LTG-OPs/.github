## About

**LTG-OPs** is the engineering organization behind the digital commerce platforms of the **Leuchtturm Gruppe** — a family-owned group of premium brands rooted in quality, craftsmanship and long-term thinking.

We build and operate modern, API-driven e-commerce solutions with a focus on:

- **Headless storefront architecture**
- **Scalable commerce backends**
- **Shared tooling and developer experience**
- **Operational reliability and maintainability**

## Engineering mindset

We prefer solutions that are:

- **Clear** over clever
- **Stable** over noisy
- **Pragmatic** over overengineered
- **Built for real operations**, not just demos

Quality in code — just like in everything else we do.
